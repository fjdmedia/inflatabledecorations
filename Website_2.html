No. 03 — The Artist

Behind the balloons.

Inflatable Decorations was founded in Winnipeg in 2022 by a balloon artist who fell in love with the craft through wedding florals and editorial styling. What began as two garlands for a friend’s bridal shower has grown into a small studio composing installations for weddings, showers, birthdays and brand events across the city.

Every piece is color-matched in the studio, hand-tied by the owner, and installed personally on site — no subcontractors, no templates. If it can be photographed, it’s worth getting right.

— With love, the Studio
